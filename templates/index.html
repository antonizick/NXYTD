Advanced Search Syntax

Duration Operators

duration > 300 — Videos longer than 5 minutes

duration < 60 — Videos shorter than 1 minute

duration >= 600 & duration <= 1800 — 10-30 minutes

Boolean Operators

& — AND (both conditions must match)

| — OR (either condition matches)

! — NOT (negate a condition)

Date Filters

upload_date > now-7d — Last 7 days

upload_date > now-365d — Last year

Title Filters

title *= rock — Contains "rock" (case-insensitive)

!title *= DJ — Does NOT contain "DJ"

Examples

duration > 300 & view_count > 1000000
Longer than 5 min AND over 1M views

duration < 300 | duration > 1200
Very short OR very long videos

title *= rock & title *= Remix & !title *= DJ
Contains "rock" and "Remix" but NOT "DJ"

Note: If Advanced Filter is filled, it overrides the filter dropdowns above.
